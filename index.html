Contributed to the front-end design and development of BottleKeep, a Japan-based platform currently under development. The platform helps bars and customers manage partially consumed alcohol bottles stored in bar lockers. Customers can later reclaim and consume their remaining bottle quantity at another branch of the same bar company.

Core Concept:
  • Bottle storage management
  • Cross-branch bottle tracking
  • Consumption record maintenance
  • Customer bottle balance management
